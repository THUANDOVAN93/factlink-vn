Fact Link is a portal site for manufacturing industry in Vietnam.
We are always looking for information on manufacturing related companies based in Vietnam.

Steps for posting company information on Fact-Link Vietnam.
① Please register as a member from the form below.
② ID and Password will be issued and sent to the registered e-mail address.
③ Log in with ID and Password, and create / edit your company page from the “Page Management” menu on the management screen. Click here for the manual (https://fact-link.com.vn/manual).
④Your company page will be published.

If you have any questions regarding registration, please contact info@fact-link.com.vn.
